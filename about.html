About Abha International Inc

Leading the way in innovative fragrance and flavor solutions

Our Story

Abha International Inc has been at the forefront of creating exceptional fragrances and flavors for nearly three decades. Our journey began with a simple vision: to blend artistry with science in creating sensory experiences that enrich lives.

Today, we are proud to serve clients across the globe, providing innovative solutions for various industries including personal care, home care, and food & beverage sectors.

Our state-of-the-art research facilities and dedicated team of experts work tirelessly to develop unique formulations that meet the evolving needs of our customers while maintaining the highest standards of quality and sustainability.

Our Company Outlets

Abha International Inc maintains a strong presence across multiple strategic locations, ensuring efficient service delivery to our valued customers. Our outlets are equipped with modern facilities and staffed by knowledgeable professionals.

Our main manufacturing facility and corporate office are located in Kanpur, with additional distribution centers strategically placed to serve different regions effectively.

Each outlet maintains our high standards of quality control and customer service, providing local access to our complete range of fragrances, flavors, and ingredients. Our distribution network ensures timely delivery and personalized support to meet your specific requirements.
